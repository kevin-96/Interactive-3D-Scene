Welcome to this interactive scene! Use the following keys to interact with the scene:

Camera:
'w' = Move forward
's' = Move backward
'a' = Move left (side to side)
'd' = Move right (side to side)
'q' = Look left
'e' = Look right
'z' = Look down
'c' = Look up
'x' = Look straight ahead (Set gaze level straight)
'r' = Go back to starting position
